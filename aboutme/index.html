About Me!

Alfonso Scudiero

I would like to share a bit about myself. My name is Alfonso, and I originally hail from Venezuela. For the past six years, I have been residing in the picturesque city of Provo, Utah, USA.

My journey to the United States wasn't without its hurdles. When I arrived here, I had no command over the English language, making my early days quite challenging. However, I was determined to overcome this barrier. I dedicated myself to learning English by attending school and gradually improving my language skills.

My thirst for knowledge and personal growth eventually led me to Ensign College, where I pursued and earned an Associate degree in Global Supply Chain. It was during my time at college that I had the incredible fortune of meeting the love of my life. Two years ago, I had the incredible privilege of marrying my beautiful wife in the sacred surroundings of the Payson Temple, marking the beginning of a beautiful journey together.

Recently, I decided to embark on a new adventure - learning web programming. I have always been drawn to challenges, and I see immense potential in this field. I am excited about this journey of growth and discovery, and I am eager to learn from each of you. Wishing all of you the very best during this semester, and I look forward to the knowledge and experiences we will share.

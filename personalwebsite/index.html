Project Management

I am dedicated to meticulous project planning, resource management, and ensuring successful project delivery, fostering client satisfaction. During my tenure with IETELMARIN, I took the lead in orchestrating the installation of 350 Transas electronic chart displays on PDVSA ships navigating Maracaibo Lake. This transformative project aimed not only at modernizing navigational tools but also revolutionizing navigation and operational procedures for heightened efficiency.
An outstanding accomplishment showcasing my project management prowess is the successful implementation of the PDVSA VTS project, where I directed the installation of 16 Automatic Power hydro meteorological buoys in Maracaibo Lake. The project not only enhanced client satisfaction but also reaffirmed my dedication to excellence across all aspects of project planning and execution. This experience underscores my commitment to optimize operational procedures within maritime environments.
